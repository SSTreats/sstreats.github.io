Pies

Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

Pie Dough:

1 1/3 cups all purpose flour

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup butter

3 1/2 tablespoons cold water

Filling:

2 cups mashed, cooked pumpkin

1 can (12 fluid oz) evaporated milk

2 eggs, beaten

3/4 cup brown sugar

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1/2 teaspoon salt

Dough Directions

1. In a medium bowl, mix 2 cups flour and the salt.

2. Using your fingers cut and mash in the butter until the butter pieces are the size of peas.

3. Sprinkle with cold water, 1 tablespoon at a time, tossing with a fork until all flour is moistened.

4. Shape dough into a ball.

5. Roll dough on lightly floured surface, using a rolling pin, into a cirlce 2 inches larger than the 9 inch pie pan.

6. Place dough into the bottom of the pie pan.

Filling Directions

1. In a large bowl, beat pumpkin with evaporated milk, eggs, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and salt with an electric mixer. Mix well.

2. Pour mixture into the prepared pie crust.

 

Bake

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

Bake 40 minutes in the preheated oven or until when a toothpick is inserted in the middle, it comes out clean.

Cool for 5 to 10 mintues before serving.
