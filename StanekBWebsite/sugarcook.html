Cookies

Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

2 3/4 cups all purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 1/4 cups butter

2 cups white sugar

2 eggs

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1/4 cup white sugar for topping

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease baking sheet..

2. In medium bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and salt.

3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and 2 cups sugar until light and fluffy.

4. Mix in the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla.

5. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients until just blended.

6. Roll dough into small balls and roll them in the 1/4 cup of white sugar.

7. Place the cookies 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet and flatten slightly.

Bake

Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned at the edges.

Cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes and remove to wire racks to cool completely.
