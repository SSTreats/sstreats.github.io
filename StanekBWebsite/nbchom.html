Chocolate Mousse Pie

Ingredients

Chocolate Mousse:

4 cups heavy cream

1 bag (12 oz) of semi sweet of dark chocolate chips

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Crust:

20 Oreos

3/4 cup butter

Crust Directions

1. Put Oreos in a large zip lock bag and crush them with a rolling pin until very fine crumbs form.

2. Pour crumbs into a medium bowl. Melt the butter and add to crumbs until mixed well.

3. Press the crumb mixture into a 9 inch pie pan making sure the crust is flat.

Mousse Directions

1. Melt the chocolate stirring occasionally. Allow to cool slightly. (not completely)

2. Whip the heavy cream until it begins to thicken. Add vanilla and whip for another 30 seconds.

3. Gently fold the warm chocolate into the whipped cream,

4. Pour the mixture on to of the Oreo crust.

5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
