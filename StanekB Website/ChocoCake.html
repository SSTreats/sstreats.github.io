Chocoloate Cake

Ingredients

2 cups white sugar

1 3/4 cups all purpose flour

3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

2 eggs

1 cup milk

1/2 cup vegetable oil

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1 cup boiling water

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease your 13 x 9 pan.

2. In large bowl stir together dry ingredients.

3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanillia and mix with a mixer for 2 minutes.

4. Stir in boiling water last.

5. Pour evenly into the prepared pan.

Bake

Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven until cake is done or when a toothpick is inserted and is removed without batter.

Cool in pan for 10 minutes then remove to cool before adding frosting or toppings.
