Cakes

Blueberry Cake

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter

1/2 cup white sugar

1/4 cup white sugar

1 tablespoon white sugar

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 egg yolks

2 egg whites

1 1/2 cups all purpose flour

1 tablespoon all purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/3 cup milk

1 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour your 8 x 8 pan.

2. Cream butter and 1/2 cup of sugar until fluffy. Add salt and vanillia. Seperate eggs and reserve the whites. Add the egg yolks to the sugar mixture and beat until creamy.

3. Combine 1 1/2 cups flour and baking powder. Add milk and egg yolk mixture. Coat berries with 1 tablespoon flour and add to batter.

4. In a seperate bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Add 1/4 cup of sugar in small amounts at a time and beat until soft peaks form. Fold egg whites into batter.

5. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle top with the remaining 1 tablespoon sugar.

Bake

Bake 50 minutes in the preheated oven until cake is done or when a toothpick is inserted and is removed without batter.
