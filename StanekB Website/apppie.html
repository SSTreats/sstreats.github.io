Pies

Apple Pie

Ingredients

Pie Dough:

2 cups all purpose flour

1 teaspoon salt

2/3 cups butter

2 tablespoons butter

4 to 6 tablespoons cold water

Filling:

6 cups thinly sliced, peeled apples

3/4 cup sugar

2 tablespoons all purpose flour

3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1 tablespoon lemon juice

Dough Directions

1. In a medium bowl, mix 2 cups flour and the salt.

2. Using your fingers cut and mash in the butter until the butter pieces are the size of peas.

3. Sprinkle with cold water, 1 tablespoon at a time, tossing with a fork until all flour is moistened.

4. Gather dough into a ball and divide it in half. Shape into 2 round balls.

5. Refrigerate for about 45 mintues.

6. Once chilled, roll dough on lightly floured surface, using a rolling pin, into cirlces 2 inches larger than the 9 inch pie pan.

 

Filling Directions

1. In a large bowl, gently mix the filling ingredients.

2. Be sure to evenly coat the apples.

 

Assmbling Instructions

1. Line the bottom of a 9 in pie pan with one circle of dough, pressing firmly against the bottom and the sides of the pan.

2.Spoon the filling into the dough-lined pie pan.

3. Cover with top dough and seal together with bottom dough.

4. Cut slits into the top dough.

5. Brush the dough with egg and sprinkle sugar on top.

Bake

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.

Bake 35 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown.

Cool for 5 to 10 mintues before serving.
