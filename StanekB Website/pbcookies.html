Cookies

Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

1 cup creamy peanut butter

1 cup brown sugar

1 cup white sugar

1 cup butter

2 eggs

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon baking powder

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 1/2 cups all purpose flour

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease baking sheet..

2. Cream butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well.

3. Add baking soda, baking powder, and vanilla to the mixture.

4. Stir in the flour.

5. Roll batter into balls and roll in sugar and place on baking sheet.

6.Flatten cookies using a fork in a criss-cross pattern

Bake

Bake 6 to 8 minutes in the preheated oven or until golden brown..

Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes and remove to wire racks to cool completely.
